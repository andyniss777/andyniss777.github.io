As COO at JinAn Digital, ran a ten-person team across the full chain — from standardized risk products to custom model delivery. Over the past year, I've been turning judgment into systems with Claude Code and LLM agents: a presales recommendation agent, an open-source modeling knowledge base called Kaggle2KnowHow, and a personal AI assistant that runs itself, daily.
